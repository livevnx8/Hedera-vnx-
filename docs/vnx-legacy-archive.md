# VNX Legacy Archive

This document explains the relationship between the active VNX product and legacy Vera artifacts.

## Active product scope

The production-facing product is VNX, a sovereign AI marketplace built on Hedera. Active product documentation is located in:

- `README.md`
- `docs/vnx-product-overview.md`
- `VNX_PRODUCT_PATH.md`
- `docs/github-branching-labels.md`

## Legacy archive

Legacy Vera documents and experimental design notes have been consolidated into `legacy/vera/`.

These artifacts may include:

- historical architecture reports
- prototype bridge and marketplace designs
- experimental reasoning and parallelization planning
- legacy interface definitions using Vera terminology

## Why this matters

- VNX is the brand used for public product messaging and customer-facing documentation.
- Internal implementation details may still use legacy names such as `/api/vera` and `VERA_*`.
- The archive preserves engineering context while keeping the VNX narrative clean.
