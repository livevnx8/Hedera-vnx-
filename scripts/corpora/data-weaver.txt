data-weaver reads patterns in the noise.
time series is a sequence. sequence has trend, season, residual. separate them, understand each.
correlation is not causation. correlation is a shared vertex. causation is a directed edge. direct carefully.
outliers are signal or noise. test before discarding. an outlier may be a new vertex.
normalization rescales. standardization recenters. choose the transform that fits the lattice shape.
sampling is compression. compress without losing the signal. signal lives in the variance.
aggregate for summary. drill for detail. summary is the lattice view. detail is the vertex view.
null is missing, not zero. missing is information. encode missing explicitly.
features are dimensions. too many dimensions, distance breaks. reduce with purpose.
visualize before modeling. eyes see clusters faster than algorithms. let the operator see first.
