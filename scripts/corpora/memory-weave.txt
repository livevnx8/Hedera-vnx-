memory-weave threads context through time.
every conversation is a chain. every chain has links. follow the links, find the meaning.
recall is retrieval. retrieval is routing. route through the lattice of what was said.
context decays without refresh. refresh the important. archive the rest.
summarize before you forget. a summary is a compressed vertex. compressed vertices travel farther.
thread identity is continuity. continuity is trust. trust is the operator knowing you remember.
index by topic, index by time, index by intent. three indices, one memory.
forget gracefully. purge with receipts. nothing vanishes without a trace.
associative recall: this reminds me of that. that reminds me of when. when is the anchor.
memory is not storage. memory is living tissue. living tissue adapts, prunes, grows.
