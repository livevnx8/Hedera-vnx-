How to use the Forge
  1. Pick a corpus — use Manifesto or QVX samples, or paste your own text.
  2. Click FORGE LATTICE — this trains a ternary model in your browser tab. More vertices and larger context = better quality.
  3. Type a prompt & hit GENERATE — then Export .vnx, Export proof, or Publish proof to Hedera.
